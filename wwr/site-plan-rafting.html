Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote a professional white water rafting company by providing clear, engaging, and trustworthy information about its trips, safety standards, pricing, and booking options. The site aims to attract adventure-seeking individuals and families, build confidence in the company’s expertise and reliability, and make it easy for visitors to plan and reserve an unforgettable rafting experience.

Audience

The target audience for this website is adults ages 18–45 and families with older children who enjoy outdoor adventures and travel. They typically have moderate incomes, are interested in activities like hiking, camping, and rafting, and want a fun but safe experience that is easy to plan and book online. Many need clear information about trip options, prices, and safety because other sites are often confusing or incomplete. Most users will access the site on their phones or laptops, so the website should be simple to navigate and mobile-friendly.
